My Story

I'm an Instructional Designer and eLearning Developer with over 7 years of experience creating SCORM-compliant, ADA-accessible learning experiences across Healthcare, Retail, Banking, Education, Manufacturing, and more.

My work spans the full development lifecycle — from needs analysis and storyboarding through to custom JavaScript interactions, AR/VR development with ZapWorks, 3D with Blender, animated video with Vyond, and multi-language localisation across 16–22 languages.

I've conducted training sessions for current and aspiring eLearning developers — sharing best practices, tools, and field-tested insights. I don't just build courses. I build the capability of teams around me.
